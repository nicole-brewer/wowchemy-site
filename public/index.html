Biography

I am a software engineer in Research Computing at Purdue University. I work in the scientific solutions group where I develop research software and science gateways. Currently I spend my time developing the backend the geospacial science gateway, myGeoHub, and developing a user interface for statistical power analysis software I am co-chair of Women in HPC at Purdue which is a proud member of the international Women in HPC organization.
